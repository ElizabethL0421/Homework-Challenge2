About

NITRO+CHiRAL

The company was formed in 2004 and released their first work, Togainu no Chi, in 2005.

Nitro Chiral, currently stylized as NITRO CHiRAL, formerly stylized as Nitro+CHiRAL, is a subsidiary of the Japanese visual novel company Nitroplus that focuses on creating BL eroge visual novels.
